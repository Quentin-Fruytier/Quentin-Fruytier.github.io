Qwent‑Finance
 		Qwent-Finance
Self-hosted · Python / Flask · Chronos-2 (GPU) · Gemini AI council

A fully local stock-trend visualizer that puts Chronos-2 probabilistic forecast cones next to momentum and moving-average signals, backs every claim with an honest, adversarially-verified backtest suite, and convenes an AI investment council that deliberates and issues a target allocation. Everything runs on my own hardware — no data leaves the machine.
